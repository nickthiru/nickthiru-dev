# Write Business Blog Post

## Prerequisites

- Clear business topic or decision to share
- Real metrics or data points (even if rounded)
- Personal experience with the business challenge
- Understanding of transparency boundaries (see `rules/content-strategy.md`)

## Steps

### 1. Topic Validation & Transparency Check

- Does this help other founders/operators?
- What's the key insight or framework?
- What specific metrics can I share?
- Review privacy guidelines in `rules/content-strategy.md`
- Frame: What I chose to share, why, and what I held back

### 2. Gather Data & Metrics

- Revenue numbers (rounded: "$5K MRR" not "$5,234.12")
- Growth percentages, conversion rates
- Time/cost savings
- Time period, market conditions, decision constraints

### 3. Create Outline

Use `src/content/templates/business-template.md` structure:

1. **Hook**: The scary/exciting decision point
2. **Transparency Decision**: What I decided to share publicly and why
3. **What I Shared (The Data)**: Specific metrics with context
4. **Business Impact**: What happened after sharing
5. **The Scary Part**: What made me nervous, what could have gone wrong
6. **What Actually Happened**: Real outcomes (good and bad)
7. **Guidelines for Others**: Framework or mental model, practical advice

### 4. Write First Draft

- Follow outline exactly
- Apply voice from `rules/voice-and-style.md`
- Include specific metrics and data points throughout
- Provide frameworks and mental models
- Share both successes and failures transparently
- Word count: 800-1500 words

### 5. Apply Voice & Style Rewrite

Use `content-creator` skill Step 3 process:

- Strategic yet practical tone
- Data-informed but not academic
- Framework-oriented thinking
- Honest about tradeoffs

### 6. SEO Optimization

Apply `rules/seo-and-quality.md` checklist.

### 7. Add Engagement Elements

- Engagement question about reader's experience
- Newsletter CTA
- Links to related business posts

### 9. Distribution

- **LinkedIn**: Strategic insights and frameworks (150-300 words)
- **X/Twitter**: Key metrics and decisions thread
- **Newsletter**: Behind-the-scenes business journey
