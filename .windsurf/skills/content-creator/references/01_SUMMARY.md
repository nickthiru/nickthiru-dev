# Summary

## Identity

`content-creator` is the foundational content skill for nickthiru.dev. It defines Nick Thiru's authentic voice, the 4-step content creation pipeline, content categories, and privacy policies. All other content skills (blog-writer, content-planner) depend on the voice and policies defined here.

## Scope

It defines:

- Authentic voice characteristics and writing style
- Universal post structure (Hook → Problem → Failed Attempts → Solution → Results → Lessons → CTA)
- Content categories: engineering (60%), business (30%), product (10%)
- 4-step content creation pipeline (Ideation → Draft → Style Rewrite → Your Edits)
- Privacy and sharing policies for "building in public" content
- Teaching style and signature phrases

It does NOT:

- Write or publish blog posts (use `blog-writer`)
- Plan content calendars or daily capture (use `content-planner`)
- Handle SEO optimization or quality checklists (use `blog-writer`)
- Manage cross-platform distribution (use `content-planner`)

## Constraints

- Voice characteristics are non-negotiable invariants — they apply to all content
- Privacy policies override content value — never share exploitable details
- The 4-step pipeline is the canonical execution path for all content creation
- Content categories determine template selection, not skill selection
