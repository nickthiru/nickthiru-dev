# Policies

## Always

- Always follow the daily capture structure: morning check-in, during-development logging, end-of-day post drafting.
- Always use build log templates (Feature, Bug/Failure, User Insight) for structured capture.
- Always select the strongest theme from the week's build logs for weekly synthesis.
- Always enforce the content mix: 60% technical, 30% business, 10% personal.
- Always apply `content-creator` voice policies before any content is published.
- Always apply `content-creator` privacy framework before sharing any metrics or details.
- Always keep cross-workspace integration manual — no automatic triggers.
- Always track performance metrics at short, medium, and long-term horizons.

## Never

- Never skip the daily capture routine — consistency is the engine.
- Never automate cross-workspace triggers between thiru-ai-labs and nickthiru-dev.
- Never publish content that hasn't passed through `content-creator` voice policies.
- Never share content that violates `content-creator` privacy framework.
- Never process files from `src/content/todo/` unless the user explicitly mentions specific files.
- Never sacrifice content quality for posting frequency.

## Content Pillars

1. **Technical Insights**: AI implementation patterns, architecture decisions, challenges.
2. **Business Impact**: ROI case studies, cost optimization, productivity gains.
3. **Building in Public**: Development journey, lessons learned, transparency stories.
4. **Practical Applications**: Real-world use cases, implementation guides.
5. **Industry Trends**: AI market analysis, competitive landscape.

## Distribution Rules

| Platform | Timing | Format | Length |
|---|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn | Tue–Thu, 8–10 AM | Professional insights, case studies | 150–300 words |
| X/Twitter | Multiple daily, 9–11 AM / 2–4 PM | Quick tips, threads, live updates | 1 tweet or 7–10 thread |
| Blog | Weekly | Long-form deep dives, guides | 800–1500 words |
| Newsletter | Thursday evening | Exclusive behind-the-scenes | Variable |

## Performance Metrics

### Short-Term (1–3 months)

- Content creation consistency.
- Engagement rates across platforms.
- Technical follower growth.

### Medium-Term (3–6 months)

- Lead generation from content.
- Thought leadership establishment.
- Content authority and backlinks.

### Long-Term (6–12 months)

- Business opportunities from content.
- Partnership and collaboration opportunities.
- Sustainable content production system.
