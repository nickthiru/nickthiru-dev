# Weekly Content Planning Workflow

## Overview

This workflow structures the 60-120 minute weekly process for creating canonical blog content and repurposing across platforms for maximum reach and impact.

## When to Use

- Weekly planning session for strategic content creation
- When you have build log content to synthesize into deeper insights
- For creating SEO-optimized blog posts that drive long-term traffic
- To maintain consistent content schedule across all platforms

## Weekly Process (60-120 minutes)

### Phase 1: Review & Theme Selection (15-20 minutes)

**Review Week's Build Logs:**

- Scan all build log entries from the past week
- Identify strongest themes or patterns
- Look for connected insights or lessons
- Note any significant milestones or breakthroughs

**Select Weekly Theme:**

- Choose one compelling story or insight
- Ensure it has broader relevance beyond just your process
- Verify you have enough detail for a full post
- Check if it aligns with your audience's interests

**Theme Selection Questions:**

- What problem did I solve that others might face?
- What mistake could help others avoid wasting time?
- What insight changed my approach to building?
- What story shows the reality of building in public?

### Phase 2: Outline Creation (10-15 minutes)

**Blog Post Outline Structure:**

1. **Hook** - Personal struggle or surprising moment
2. **Problem Context** - What I was trying to accomplish
3. **Failed Attempts** - What didn't work and why
4. **Breakthrough Moment** - The key insight or change
5. **Step-by-Step Solution** - How I actually solved it
6. **Results & Metrics** - What actually happened
7. **Lessons Learned** - Broader insights and takeaways
8. **What I'd Do Differently** - Practical advice for others

**Outline Template:**

```markdown
# [Working Title]

**Hook:** [Personal opening that grabs attention]

**Problem:** [What I was trying to achieve and why it mattered]

**Failed Attempts:**

- [First attempt and why it failed]
- [Second attempt and lessons learned]

**Breakthrough:** [The key insight that changed everything]

**Solution Steps:**

1. [Step 1 with specific details]
2. [Step 2 with code/examples]
3. [Step 3 with outcomes]

**Results:** [Actual metrics and outcomes]

**Lessons:** [Broader takeaways and insights]

**Different Next Time:** [What others should do differently]
```

### Phase 3: Content Creation (30-60 minutes)

**Write the Blog Post:**

- Follow your outline exactly
- Write in your authentic voice (refer to voice guide)
- Include specific numbers, dates, and details
- Add code examples or screenshots where relevant
- Ensure SEO optimization (keywords, meta description)
- Word count: 800-1500 words for optimal depth

**SEO Checklist:**

- [ ] **Meta description** (150-160 characters, includes keywords)
- [ ] **Title tag** (50-60 characters, compelling)
- [ ] **Headings hierarchy** (H1, H2, H3 structure)
- [ ] **Internal links** to relevant content
- [ ] **Keywords included naturally** in headings and content
- [ ] **Mobile-friendly formatting** and readability

**Voice Authenticity Checklist:**

- [ ] **Personal story** in opening hook
- [ ] **Specific details** (dates, numbers, metrics)
- [ ] **Vulnerability** about struggles or mistakes
- [ ] **"I" statements** throughout for authenticity
- [ ] **Conversational tone** like talking to a friend
- [ ] **Practical takeaways** for readers

### Phase 5: Scheduling & Distribution (5-10 minutes)

**Scheduling Strategy:**

- **Blog post**: Publish immediately, then share
- **LinkedIn**: Schedule for 8-10 AM tomorrow
- **X/Twitter**: Schedule for 9-11 AM tomorrow
- **Newsletter**: Send Thursday evening (if weekly schedule)

## SEO & Content Strategy

### Keyword Research

- **Primary keywords**: 2-3 main topics per post
- **Secondary keywords**: Related terms and concepts
- **Long-tail keywords**: Specific questions and problems
- **Competitor analysis**: What's ranking for similar topics

### Content Pillars

Create content around these core themes:

1. **AI Agent Development** - Technical tutorials and insights
2. **Building in Public** - Transparency and audience growth
3. **Solo Founder Journey** - Business lessons and mindset
4. **Product Development** - Process and strategy
5. **Technical Leadership** - Architecture and decision-making

### Internal Linking Strategy

- Link to 2-3 relevant previous posts
- Use descriptive anchor text
- Create topic clusters around content pillars
- Update old posts with links to new content

## Quality Assurance

### Pre-Publishing Checklist

- [ ] **Content accuracy**: All claims verified, code tested
- [ ] **Voice consistency**: Sounds like you, not generic
- [ ] **SEO optimization**: All elements in place
- [ ] **Formatting**: Clean, readable, mobile-friendly
- [ ] **Links**: All internal/external links working
- [ ] **Images**: Optimized with alt text
- [ ] **Call to action**: Clear next step for readers

### Post-Publishing Review

- [ ] **Social shares**: Posts distributed correctly
- [ ] **Engagement**: Respond to comments promptly
- [ ] **Analytics**: Track performance metrics
- [ ] **Feedback**: Note audience questions and suggestions
- [ ] **Optimization**: Update based on performance data

## Performance Tracking

### Key Metrics to Monitor

- **Blog traffic**: Page views, time on page, bounce rate
- **Search rankings**: Keyword positions, organic traffic
- **Social engagement**: Likes, comments, shares, clicks
- **Newsletter metrics**: Open rates, click rates, unsubscribes
- **Business impact**: Waitlist signups, customer inquiries

### Weekly Review Questions

- Which content theme resonated most with audience?
- What questions did people ask in comments?
- Which platform drove the most traffic?
- How can I improve content based on feedback?
- What topics should I explore more deeply?

## Common Challenges & Solutions

### Challenge: "I don't have enough content for a full post"

**Solution**: Combine related build log entries, expand on smaller insights, or create a "lessons learned" roundup from the week.

### Challenge: "My content isn't getting traffic"

**Solution**: Focus on SEO optimization, internal linking, and promoting content in relevant communities. Consistency compounds over time.

### Challenge: "I'm running out of topic ideas"

**Solution**: Your build log is endless content. Also look at audience questions, competitor content, and industry trends for inspiration.

### Challenge: "Content creation takes too much time"

**Solution**: Use the templates and systems outlined here. Batch similar tasks together and leverage your daily build log capture.
