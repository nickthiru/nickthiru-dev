# Why OpsPilot: The Problem I'm Solving

Every morning, I open Slack, GitHub, Jira, and three other dashboards just to understand what's happening. The problem isn't lack of information — it's too much information scattered across too many places.

## The Problem

Ops teams spend more time gathering context than acting on it. A PR sits in GitHub, an alert fires in PagerDuty, a stakeholder asks about it in Slack — and nobody has the full picture in one place.

## The Vision

What if there was a single source of truth that pulled together:

- Alerts and incidents
- PRs and deployments
- Team signals and status updates
- All in one unified dashboard

That's OpsPilot.
