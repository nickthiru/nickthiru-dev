# Why PolicyForge: The Compliance Gap I Discovered

I talked to several founders about their security audit preparation. The pattern was consistent: weeks of work, expensive consultants, and policies that were copy-pasted from templates nobody understood.

## The Problem

Every company needs security policies for SOC 2, ISO 27001, or similar frameworks. The process is:

1. Hire a consultant ($10k-$50k)
2. Wait 4-8 weeks
3. Get a document nobody on the team understands
4. Repeat annually

## The Vision

What if you could:

- Answer 15-20 questions about your tech stack
- Get framework-aligned security policies in minutes
- Understand every policy because it's written for your specific context

That's PolicyForge.
