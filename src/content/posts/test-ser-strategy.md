# Why I'm Building Social Engagement Radar

I've spent countless hours scrolling through LinkedIn and X, looking for conversations where I could add value. The problem isn't finding conversations — it's finding the _right_ conversations at the _right_ time.

## The Problem

Every day, thousands of people ask questions, share frustrations, or seek recommendations related to topics I care about. But these conversations are scattered across platforms, buried in feeds, and disappear quickly. By the time I find them, the moment has passed.

## The Opportunity

What if I could:

- Get notified when someone asks a question I can answer?
- Have AI draft a thoughtful response based on my voice and experience?
- Review and send it with one click?

That's the core idea behind Social Engagement Radar.
